About Us

This is a portal where people can share their things with their fellow residents, and give their small bit in reducing the pollution caused by carbon emissions. We here focus at reducing the use of few consumer products whose production causes environmental pollution, we aim to spread our portal across different apartment complexes, society and small villages. We allow the members to list their products which can be used by the fellow residents collectively. The users can give items, request items, use it and then return the items. We aim to reduce the consumption of products from the market which will eventually reduce the production of such items and hence will contribute in reducing the carbon emissions.
